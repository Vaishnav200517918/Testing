ABOUT ME

V.R. Ajith Kumar Born in Karunagappally, Kollam, Kerala, India in 1960, V.R. Ajith Kumar is the son of V. Raghavan Pillai and P. Shanthamma. He has authored several Malayalam novels, including Eechaakalum Urumbukalum Janadhipathyam Kothikkumbol (When Flies and Ants Crave Democracy), Ozhukku (The Flow), Ormanoolukal (Memory Threads), Pakothi Padayani (a mystic novel), and Kedatha Chita (The Burning Pyre).
His contributions to children's literature include Aana Katha (The Elephant Story), Sasneham Nitavo (With Love, Nitavo), Januvinte Katha (The Story of Janu), Sreekuttante Delhi (Sreekuttan’s Delhi), and Babukkuvinte Athbhutha Lokam (Babuku’s Wonderful World). He has also penned C. Kesavante Jeevacharithram (A Short Biography of C. Kesavan), Ambedkarrum India Vibhajanavum (Ambedkar and the Partition of India), and a critical analysis of Chanakya Neethi.
Ajith Kumar has written around a hundred short stories and nearly fifty documentary and telefilm scripts. He also directed a documentary on Dr. Leela Omchery.
He has held several prominent positions, including Additional Director in the Information and Public Relations Department, Registrar of Malayalam Mission, Secretary of the Kerala Media Academy, Head of Kite Victors Channel, Director of Akshaya, Information Officer and Deputy Director at Delhi Kerala House. Additionally, he served as Editor of Agriculture World and Krishi Jagaran magazines.
